Who We Are

Our passion and identity make Nebras Films unique. We are the only full service movie production studio in Saudi Arabia. Our state-of-the-art equipment and capabilities are unparalleled anywhere in the Middle East.

Our Group

Alnafees Holding Company, the parent company of Nebras Films, specializes in pioneering growth companies through its various activities and investments aligned with Saudi Arabia’s Vision 2030 transformation plan.

Pioneering Approach

We identify story concepts using global and regional trends combined with our own creative talent. We source the best expertise and team it with the latest technologies to transform those stories into motion pictures that showcase the best of the Middle East.
