What is Shared Hosting best for?

Shared Hosting plans offer the most affordable way to get a site online — because the costs of maintaining a server are split among multiple users. This style of hosting is ideal for every startup, small business, or personal website.

What’s the difference between Managed Hosting and Shared Hosting?

Managed WordPress Hosting is a hosting environment that is specifically designed for WordPress, which doesn’t require any maintenance on your part. With Shared Hosting, you share your resources with other website owners. This means that if someone else’s website gets a lot of traffic, you may experience downtime. With Managed Hosting you’ll also never notice if a server or any hardware fails because your website stays containerised from such incidents.

How does Reseller Hosting work?

When you select your desired plan, you are provided with access via cPanel to your Web Host Manager (WHM) program. This is where you can create and manage all your reseller accounts on the server, which other resellers might occupy. (Keep in mind all created accounts are referred to as “resold accounts.”) Your reseller privileges are determined by your hosting provider, i.e. CloudX.
