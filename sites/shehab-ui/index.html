Our Story

Day by day Shehab’s family is continuously growing and evolving.

  • 1

    It all started back in 1970 in Damanhur.

    The time of our Great Grandfather Shehab (first generation) humble beginning as a meat distributor, He used to buy cattles and calves from the farmers and sell them in the cities of Damanhur and Alexandria.

  • 2

    Then the second generation

    started expanding The business with having more and larger farms, then started supplying to Al Beheira and Alexandria, then opening our first store in Alexandria in 1990, and year after year our experience became greater and business went bigger now we’re The lead in the meat industry based in Alexandria.

3

In 2000s we’ve rebranded our company

enhanced our store and services, created a new style for the butchery ( to be the first luxurious meat store in Alexandria) and added Various new products other than just meat.

4

In 2021 we had our ISO certificate

to be the first ISO certified meat store in Alexandria. we still paving our way towards our goal to be The lead Food Store, we are looking forward to expand in new locations and having new challenges.
